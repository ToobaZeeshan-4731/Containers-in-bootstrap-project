Hobbies


Hobbies are activities that people engage in for enjoyment and personal fulfillment, outside of their work or daily responsibilities. They can be creative, physical, intellectual, or social, and they offer a way to relax, explore new interests, and develop new skills. Hobbies provide a sense of purpose and help improve mental well-being by offering a break from routine stress. Pursuing hobbies allows individuals to express themselves, connect with others, and often discover new passions.
